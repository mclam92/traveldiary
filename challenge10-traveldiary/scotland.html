Scotland

Scotland might be a wee place on the map, but with so much beauty to behold and incredible places to visit, the hours will pass by in a blink of an eye. From the gorgeous scenery and rich history to the culture and the personalities of the people who live here, the unique aspects of the nation are appealing to so many around the world, but especially those in need of a bit of soul-searching under the big skies.
